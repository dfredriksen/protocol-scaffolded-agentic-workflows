# Submission Review Persona Map V1

Date: 2026-04-11  
Scope: primary-submission-readiness roles for the current Teaching manuscript

## Purpose

Make the current submission-readiness surface legible as a staffed review model rather than a single undifferentiated `VP of Research` queue.

## Role map

| Role | Primary responsibility | Governing stories | Main artifacts |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| `VP of Research` | Own venue choice, external positioning, and final research-side submission posture | `S094`, `S107` | `external-readiness-review-memo-v1.md`, `emse-cover-letter-draft-v1.md` |
| `Chief Research Officer` | Own the internal scientific review outcome and must-fix judgment before submission | `S085` | `internal-manuscript-review-memo-v1.md` |
| `Research Operations Lead` | Own review-process consistency, reviewer handoff, and operational packet movement | `S084`, `S111` | `internal-manuscript-review-checklist-v1.md`, `docs/hitl/H010-human-l2-review-solicitation-packet-v1.md` |
| `Submissions Editor` | Own submission-packet completeness and venue-facing upload readiness | `S103` | `journal-submission-packet-checklist-v1.md`, `docs/evidence/review-packets/emse-submission-v1/` |
| `Disclosure And IP Review Lead` | Own disclosure clearance and IP boundary review before submission | `S115` | controlled disclosure-clearance memo or linked review surface |

## Current operating interpretation

The manuscript now clears the document-quality threshold and has a populated EMSE title page. Under this role map, the remaining work is no longer missing evidence, missing supplement construction, or missing declarations scaffolding. It is execution of the final staffed signoff, a last visual `.docx` check, and then submission from the canonical bundle.

## Minimum signoff set before send

1. `Chief Research Officer` confirms no remaining scientific must-fix issue.
2. `Disclosure And IP Review Lead` clears submission or records carve-outs.
3. `VP of Research` approves venue-facing submission.
4. `Submissions Editor` performs the actual upload from the canonical bundle.
