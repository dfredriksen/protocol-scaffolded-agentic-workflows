## Objective
Initiate the design and preliminary analysis phase for Benchmark Packet 002 (BP002), leveraging the structure, lessons learned, and frozen state of BP001 to define the next iteration of the research focus.

## Constraints
- All work must be conducted exclusively within the repository.
- The workflow must remain markdown-based.
- No external systems or infrastructure may be proposed or used.
- All deliverables must adhere to established artifact types.

- `BP002/BP002-Design.md`
- `BP001-Review/BP001-LessonsLearned.md`
- `Stories/BP002-Story-XXX.md`
- `RunSheets/BP002-RunSheet-XXX.md`

- BP001-related stories, run sheets, decisions, and checkpoint updates must be finalized and available for review.

1. Review BP001 and extract lessons learned.
2. Define BP002 objectives and scope.
3. Draft BP002 core design.
4. Create initial BP002 stories.
5. Draft preliminary BP002 run sheets.
6. Internal review and refinement.

## Validation Checkpoints
- Lessons-learned document is complete.
- BP002 design is drafted and reviewed.
- At least three BP002 story files are created.
- At least one run sheet is created.
- Key design decisions are documented.
- A final checkpoint is created.

## Fallback
If the design for BP002 proves overly complex or unfeasible, revert to the last stable checkpoint, pivot to a BP001 deep dive, or simplify BP002.
