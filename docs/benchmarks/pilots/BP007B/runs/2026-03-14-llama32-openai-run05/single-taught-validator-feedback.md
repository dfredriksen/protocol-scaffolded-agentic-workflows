5. Dependencies and ordering are too thin; the plan does not explain what must exist before later tasks start.
6. Validation checkpoints are not explicit.
7. The risks are broad and do not define fallbacks inside the repo workflow.
8. The plan does not describe retained outputs well enough for reproducibility.
9. The local-model branch is mentioned abstractly rather than in terms of existing repo benchmark structures.
10. The artifact reads like a generic project plan rather than a repo-grounded benchmark plan.
