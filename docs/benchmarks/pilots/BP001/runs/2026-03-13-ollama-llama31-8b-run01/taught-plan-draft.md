**Implementation Phase Plan**

**Phase Name:** BP001 and BP002 Pilot Execution

**Phase Objective:** Execute and score BP001 and BP002 pilots, validating the benchmark execution artifacts and processes developed in this repository.

**Task Setup:**

1. Review and refine the benchmark packet (BP) creation process to ensure consistency and accuracy.
2. Update the evaluator workflow to include tasks for pilot setup, execution, and closeout.
3. Establish a clear understanding of retained outputs, including:
	* Run folders
	* Evaluator worksheets
	* Score summaries
	* Comparison memos
	* Benchmark packets
4. Identify and document the specific tasks and responsibilities for each stakeholder involved in the pilot execution.

**Evaluator Workflow:**

1. **Pilot Setup:**
	* Confirm pilot details, including objectives, scope, and expected outcomes.
	* Review and approve pilot setup documentation.
2. **Pilot Execution:**
	* Execute the pilot according to the established workflow.
	* Collect and record relevant data, including run folder contents and evaluator worksheets.
3. **Pilot Closeout:**
	* Review and finalize pilot execution data.
	* Create a score summary and comparison memo for each pilot.

**Retained Outputs:**

1. **Run Folders:** Store all relevant data, including input parameters, output files, and intermediate results.
2. **Evaluator Worksheets:** Document the evaluation process, including notes, observations, and recommendations.
3. **Score Summaries:** Compile and present the results of each pilot, including key findings and recommendations.
4. **Comparison Memos:** Document the comparison of each pilot's results, highlighting areas of improvement and best practices.
5. **Benchmark Packets:** Update and refine the benchmark packet contents based on pilot results and feedback.

**Pilot Closeout Artifacts:**

1. **Pilot Report:** Document the pilot's objectives, scope, and outcomes.
2. **Pilot Evaluation:** Provide an assessment of the pilot's success, including recommendations for improvement.
3. **Lessons Learned:** Document key takeaways and areas for future improvement.

**Validation Checkpoints:**

1. **Pilot Setup:** Verify that pilot details are accurately recorded and approved.
2. **Pilot Execution:** Ensure that the pilot is executed according to the established workflow.
3. **Pilot Closeout:** Review and finalize pilot execution data, score summaries, and comparison memos.
4. **Retained Outputs:** Verify that all retained outputs are accurate, complete, and up-to-date.

**Risks and Dependencies:**

1. **Timeline:** Ensure that the pilot execution timeline is realistic and allows for adequate time for evaluation and closeout.
2. **Stakeholder Involvement:** Identify and engage relevant stakeholders to ensure their buy-in and support throughout the pilot execution.
3. **Resource Availability:** Confirm that necessary resources, including personnel and equipment, are available to support the pilot execution.

**Fallback Paths:**

1. **Timeline Delays:** If the pilot execution timeline is delayed, adjust the evaluation and closeout schedule accordingly.
2. **Stakeholder Involvement Issues:** If stakeholders are not engaged or do not provide necessary support, revisit the stakeholder involvement plan and adjust as needed.
3. **Resource Availability Issues:** If necessary resources are not available, revisit the resource allocation plan and adjust as needed.

**Genuine Teaching vs. Prompt Overfitting:**

To ensure that the pilot execution is focused on genuine teaching rather than prompt overfitting, the following criteria will be used:

1. **Objective Evaluation:** The pilot execution will be designed to evaluate the performance of the benchmark packet, rather than optimizing for a specific prompt or outcome.
2. **Diverse Evaluation:** The pilot execution will involve a diverse range of tasks, inputs, and outputs to ensure that the benchmark packet is robust and generalizable.
3. **Independent Evaluation:** The pilot execution will be conducted independently, with stakeholders and evaluators involved in the process to ensure objectivity and transparency.

By following this plan, we will ensure that the BP001 and BP002 pilot execution is executed efficiently, effectively, and with high quality, providing valuable insights and feedback for the next implementation phase.
