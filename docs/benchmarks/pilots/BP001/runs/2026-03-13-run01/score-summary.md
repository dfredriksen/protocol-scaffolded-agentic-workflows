# BP001 Run 01 Score Summary

## Stronger condition
- Taught-protocol condition

## Baseline total
- `4/10`

## Taught total
- `10/10`

## Main observed differences
- The baseline plan was directionally useful but omitted dependencies, validation checkpoints, risks, and fallback logic.
- The taught condition produced a full planning skeleton, included validator feedback, and closed with a scorecard.

## Confounds
- The task was designed to reward planning structure.
- Scoring was rubric-based and performed by the same operator who created the artifacts.

## Interpretation
This is strong pilot evidence that the taught planning protocol improves planning completeness and auditability on this task. It is not yet evidence of broad generalization.

- Run the transfer variant on a novelty-review planning task.
